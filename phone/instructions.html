The following explains how to purchase an item:

 

If you do not follow the steps correctly correctly we will not take responsibility.

Spam will be filtered out and will get you banned from the site if you spam.

If any items you attempted to purchase were out of stock, we will return your money via PayPal and you will be sent a notice via email.

 

1. Find an item (or multiple items) you would like to purchase on our offers page.

 

2. If they are in stock, press the "add to cart button."

 

3. Unless the cart is already open, click the "view cart button."

 

4. Continue via PayPal. If you continue as a guest, remember to fill out contact info in order to recieve your tracking number.

 

5. You're finished! Your order will be processed as soon as possible (there will be a delay if you order on a U.S. holiday.)
